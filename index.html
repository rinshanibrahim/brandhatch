COLLECTION NOTE

This exclusive collection has a play of graphics that bring together florals & geos with a new-age print-on-print juxtapositioning.


The colours are vibrant & playful with contrasting elements, balanced by classic neutrals. This mix-n-match of colours & patterns makes for a bold yet sophisticated statement.


Staying true to our commitment to responsible fashion, the left-over fabric has been creatively up-cycled. They have been used to create matching scrunchies & also packaging bags which, can be re-used for storage or travel.

ABOUT THE BRAND

MADE IN INDIA | WITH LOVE

An indigenous luxury fashion & lifestyle brand with a perfect blend of sustainability & elevated style.


Our hand-crafted products with authentic elegant style are designed for a discerning global audience who seek unique & responsible fashion choices.


We strive to create “investment pieces” for our customer’s wardrobe which are timeless & stay relevant irrespective of changing trends.
